About Me

Afua Ntriwaa Peprah — a passionate educator, digital strategist, and Scrum Master with a heart for service and a mind for systems. My career journey has taken me across diverse industries, from founding a nonprofit school in rural Ghana to supporting agile software teams across continents. With over 10 years of experience in education, hospitality, digital marketing, and now IT consulting, I’ve developed a unique blend of people-centered leadership and tech-forward thinking. I thrive in environments where collaboration meets creativity and where structure enables growth. Currently, I serve as a Junior Scrum Master, helping teams streamline their workflows and embrace agile principles. My technical toolkit includes tools like JIRA, Trello, GitHub, and monitoring platforms like Prometheus and Grafana. I’m also deepening my knowledge in DevOps, cloud infrastructure, and automation through hands-on projects and ongoing certifications. But beyond the tech, what drives me is impact. Whether it’s designing engaging lesson plans, building a supportive school community, or leading a team through a successful product sprint — I’m all about creating solutions that serve people.
